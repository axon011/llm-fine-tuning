About the Role
AI Engineer to turn messy inputs into structured knowledge and reliable answers. You will design and operate knowledge-graph + LLM (RAG) pipelines, model/ingest domain ontologies, and own evaluation so we can ship trustworthy features.

Key Responsibilities
- Model the domain (ontology/taxonomy), build ETL into a graph store, and author queries (SPARQL/Cypher) to surface graph facts and relationships in features
- Design retrieval and answer generation workflows (indexing, chunking, reranking), and orchestrate prompts/tools while balancing KG, vector search, and business rules
- Define and track retrieval/answer metrics (e.g., precision/recall, faithfulness)
- Own evaluation so we can ship trustworthy features
